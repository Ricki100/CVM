Commercial law that keeps your business moving

Business owners need legal advice that is fast, understandable and commercially grounded. CVM helps clients form companies, negotiate agreements, manage compliance, protect cash flow and resolve disputes before they distract from growth.

How CVM helps

  • Company registration, shareholder agreements and governance structures
  • Commercial contracts, MOUs, service agreements and joint ventures
  • Mergers, acquisitions, business sales and due diligence
  • Employment contracts, workplace policies and disciplinary processes
  • Debt collection, contract disputes and settlement negotiations
  • Regulatory compliance involving ZIDA, ZIMRA, RBZ and sector-specific authorities
Do small businesses need a lawyer before signing contracts?

Yes. Early contract review can prevent unclear payment terms, weak remedies, tax exposure, employment risk and avoidable disputes.

Can CVM help a foreign investor enter Zimbabwe?

Yes. CVM advises on subsidiaries, branches, joint ventures, investment approvals, commercial contracts and local compliance requirements.
