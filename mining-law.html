Specialist mining lawyers for Zimbabwe's mining sector

CVM advises gold, platinum, lithium, diamond and chrome sector clients on licencing, compliance, disputes and transactions. The firm combines knowledge of the Mines and Minerals Act with commercial pragmatism.

Services

  • Mining licence applications, renewals, transfers and objections
  • Special grants, exclusive prospecting orders and block mining orders
  • EMA compliance, environmental impact assessments and rehabilitation obligations
  • Surface rights negotiations and compensation claims
  • Mining joint ventures, farm-in agreements and royalty arrangements
  • MMCZ export compliance, disputes, litigation and arbitration
What lawyers handle mining licences in Zimbabwe?

CVM Legal Practice assists mining companies, exploration entities and investors with mining licence applications, renewals, transfers and compliance.

How do I obtain a mining licence in Zimbabwe?

The licence type depends on the activity. Applications are administered through mining authorities and usually require technical and financial submissions.
