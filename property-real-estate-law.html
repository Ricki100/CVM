Real estate advice for safer property decisions

Property transactions carry risk when title, taxes, rates clearance, seller authority, financing or lease terms are not handled correctly. CVM helps clients verify, transfer, protect and enforce property interests across Zimbabwe.

How CVM helps

  • Residential, commercial and agricultural property transfers
  • Deeds of Sale, offer-to-purchase agreements and lease agreements
  • Title deed searches, seller authority checks and property due diligence
  • Mortgage bonds, bond cancellations and Deeds Office lodgements
  • Landlord-tenant disputes, evictions and lease enforcement
  • Property development structuring and subdivision-related legal support
How long does a property transfer take in Zimbabwe?

A standard transfer often takes 6 to 12 weeks once documents are in order, although ZIMRA, rates clearance, bond and Deeds Office issues can affect timing.

Should I do a title deed search before buying property?

Yes. A title search helps confirm ownership, encumbrances and transaction risks before money changes hands.
