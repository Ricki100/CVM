Farmers & Agribusiness

Legal support for farmers and agribusiness in Zimbabwe.

CVM helps farmers, producers and agribusiness operators protect land interests, structure commercial relationships, resolve disputes and plan succession with clear legal guidance.

Protect your farm interestsView agriculture services

Legal advice for land, crops, labour and farm succession

Agricultural businesses often carry legal risk across land rights, supply contracts, equipment finance, employment relationships, family ownership and inheritance. CVM gives practical advice that helps farmers keep operations moving while legal issues are handled properly.

How CVM helps

  • Agricultural leases, land use agreements and farm occupation issues
  • Contract drafting for supply, offtake, input finance and equipment arrangements
  • Farm labour contracts, disciplinary matters and retrenchment processes
  • Debt recovery, payment disputes and enforcement of commercial obligations
  • Succession planning, wills, estates and family farm ownership disputes
  • Property transfers, title deed searches and due diligence for agricultural land
What legal issues do farmers commonly face in Zimbabwe?

Farmers often need help with land rights, leases, supply contracts, labour disputes, debt recovery, equipment finance, estate planning and family ownership disputes.

Can CVM help with agricultural property transfers?

Yes. CVM handles due diligence, Deeds Office processes, title checks and transfer documentation for agricultural, residential and commercial property matters.
