Corporate and commercial legal services in Zimbabwe

Zimbabwe's business environment demands legal counsel that is technically precise and commercially astute. CVM acts for start-ups, SMEs, listed companies, parastatals and international investors operating in, or entering, the Zimbabwean market.

Services

  • Company registration and incorporation under the Companies and Other Business Entities Act
  • Commercial contracts, MOUs and joint venture agreements
  • Mergers, acquisitions and business sales from due diligence to closing
  • Shareholders' agreements, partnerships and restructuring
  • ZIDA, ZIMRA and RBZ regulatory compliance
  • Corporate governance and board secretarial services
How do I register a company in Zimbabwe?

You choose a name, prepare constitutional documents, file with CIPO and complete relevant ZIMRA steps. CVM manages the process and aligns the structure with your business goals.

Can a foreign company invest in Zimbabwe?

Yes. Foreign investors can establish subsidiaries, joint ventures or branches, subject to ZIDA requirements and sector-specific approvals.
