What is CVM Legal Practice?

Where is CVM Legal Practice located?

CVM is located at 5 Sunderland Avenue, Belvedere, Harare, Zimbabwe. Office hours are Monday to Friday, 08:00 to 16:30.

What is the cost of conveyancing in Zimbabwe?

Conveyancing fees are guided by the Law Society of Zimbabwe scale and depend on the property value, transfer duty, Deeds Office fees and transaction-specific requirements.

Can a foreign company invest in Zimbabwe?

Yes. Foreign investors can establish subsidiaries, joint ventures or branches, subject to ZIDA requirements and sector-specific regulatory approvals.
