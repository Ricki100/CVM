Legal support for builders and property developers.

Construction legal advice from land acquisition to completion

Development projects depend on clean title, enforceable contracts, proper approvals, reliable payment terms and clear dispute mechanisms. CVM helps project owners and builders reduce legal risk before it becomes expensive.

How CVM helps

  • Land acquisition, property due diligence and conveyancing support
  • Construction contracts, contractor agreements and supplier terms
  • Payment disputes, debt recovery and enforcement of project obligations
  • Employment and labour advice for site teams and contractors
  • Joint ventures, development structures and investor agreements
  • Property disputes, lease issues and litigation involving development projects
Why should a developer involve lawyers early?

Early legal review helps confirm title, authority to sell, approval risks, payment security, contractor obligations and dispute remedies before project costs escalate.

Can CVM help with contractor payment disputes?

Yes. CVM assists with demand letters, negotiation, debt recovery, litigation, arbitration and settlement strategies.
