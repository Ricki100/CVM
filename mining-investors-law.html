Mining law support for licences, deals and disputes

Mining projects need careful legal handling from claim verification and licence applications to joint ventures, environmental compliance, export obligations and dispute resolution. CVM combines commercial legal strategy with knowledge of Zimbabwe mining processes.

How CVM helps

  • Mining licence applications, renewals, transfers and objections
  • Claim due diligence, title verification and transaction risk review
  • Mining joint ventures, farm-in arrangements and royalty structures
  • Surface rights negotiations, land access and compensation disputes
  • EMA compliance, EIA requirements and rehabilitation obligations
  • Mining disputes, arbitration, litigation and regulatory engagement
What legal checks should a mining investor do in Zimbabwe?

Investors should verify claim or licence status, ownership, environmental obligations, surface rights, regulatory approvals, royalties, disputes and transaction authority.

Can CVM assist with mining licence transfers?

Yes. CVM assists with licence transfer processes, documentation, due diligence, objections and related commercial agreements.
