How long does a property transfer take in Zimbabwe?

A standard property transfer in Zimbabwe typically takes 6 to 12 weeks from the date all documents are in order. CVM provides realistic timelines and transparent estimates at the outset.

Services

  • Title deed searches and due diligence
  • Mortgage bonds and cancellation of bonds
  • Property development legal structuring
  • Lease agreements, evictions and landlord-tenant disputes
What is the cost of conveyancing in Zimbabwe?

Fees are guided by the Law Society of Zimbabwe scale and depend on property value, transfer duty, stamp duty and Deeds Office fees.

What documents do I need to transfer property?

Common documents include a signed Deed of Sale, seller's title deed, ZIMRA clearance, identity documents and rates clearance.
