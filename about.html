About CVM Legal Practice

Harare's full-service law firm.

CVM Legal Practice, formally Chakawa, Vhera and Mswelanto Legal Practice, serves individuals, corporations, parastatals, mining companies and foreign investors across Zimbabwe.

Our offices are at 5 Sunderland Avenue, Belvedere, Harare.

Integrity, excellence and client commitment.

CVM has built its reputation on practical legal advice, disciplined execution and an unwavering commitment to clients. Our practitioners are registered with the Law Society of Zimbabwe and advise on both contentious and non-contentious matters.

Our philosophy

Law at its best is not about complexity. It is about clarity, strategy and outcomes. CVM cuts through legal complexity to give clients actionable advice for business, family and investment decisions.

Our location

CVM is located at 5 Sunderland Avenue, Belvedere, Harare, Zimbabwe. The firm advises clients throughout Zimbabwe and in cross-border transactions involving the Southern African region.
